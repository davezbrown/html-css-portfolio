Becoming a Developer

There are several reasons why I am learning to code. First of all, this is a career I have always had interest in. I love problem-solving, and I feel like coding is a skill that I am wired for. I have spent my schooling years and my professional years thus far pursuing careers that have been fulfilling yet financially challenging. Now that I have two sons to provide for in addition to my wife, I have decided to flip towards a new chapter. Becoming a developer now checks all of my boxes. It allows me to do something that I know I will love, while also providing for my family and enabling me to do other things that I love as well. I am very excited about the future!
